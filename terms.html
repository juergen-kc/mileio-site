Description of Service

MileIO is a fuel tracking application for iOS devices that allows you to:

  • Log vehicle fuel entries manually or via OCR scanning
  • Track fuel efficiency metrics (MPG, L/100km, kWh/100mi, cost per mile)
  • Sync data across your Apple devices using iCloud
  • Analyze fuel consumption with charts and statistics
  • Export fuel data to CSV and PDF formats
  • Track both gasoline/diesel and electric vehicle charging

The App is provided free of charge with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
